Three simple steps to collecting authenticated ORCID iDs!

Want to collect authenticated ORCID iDs from a group of researchers? Attendees at your next event, perhaps? Everyone in your research group/lab? Or others? Share My iD makes it as simple as 1, 2, 3!

  1. Create a new iD collection (all you need is your own ORCID iD!)
  2. Use the Share link to request iDs by email or on a webpage.
  3. Visit the Share link to view and download the iDs you've collected.

Not sure if everyone already has an ORCID iD? No problem! New users can register for an iD and add it to your collection at the same time.

Why authenticated ORCID iDs?

ORCID is all about correctly identifying who's who, so it's important that you get the correct iD for each person. No room for typos here!
